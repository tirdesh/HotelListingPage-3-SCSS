Overview

Located in Taksim Gmsuyu, the heart of Istanbul. the CVK Park Bosphorus Hotel Istanbul has risen from the ashes of the historic Park Hotel, which also served as Foreign Affairs 120 years ago and is hosting its guests by assuming this hospitality mission. With its 452 luxurious rooms and Suites, 8500 m2 SPA and fitness area, 18 meeting rooms including 4 dividable ones and 3 terraces With Bosphorus view, Istanbuls largest terrace with view (4500 m2) and latest intrastructre.
